How It Works

THE RULES
ARE SIMPLE

1
Spotify ranks artists daily

Every day, Spotify updates each artist's position based on monthly listeners. The top 500 show on their profile. Position 501 disappears.

2
You want to find the edge

In solo play, you're not chasing number one - you want artists sitting as close to position 500 as possible. Obvious picks score low.

3
Daily challenge tests accuracy

Three mystery artists are revealed. You guess their exact rank. The closer you get, the lower your score. Think golf, not football.

4
Share your result

Every game ends with a shareable result card showing how you did - without spoiling the artists for anyone who hasn't played yet.
